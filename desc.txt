The PostgreSQL Analyser is a focused utility crafted to analyze PostgreSQL database instances across various OpenShift Container Platform (OCP) clusters. Its primary aim is to facilitate cost optimization by scrutinizing database deployments, configurations, and resource allocations. By highlighting potential inefficiencies such as oversized resources in development environments or non-essential multi-AZ setups, the tool aids in pinpointing opportunities to scale down expenses. The web interface offers an accessible platform for users to filter data and gain insights into database distribution and cost implications, promoting efficient management and fiscal prudence in database operations.
